Summary

Project Purpose: to analysis how the weather patterns may impact on one travels across the world.

First, the data,was pulled from the Open Weather API and the CitiPy Python Library (500 cities from all over the world pulled randomly).

Afterwardes the data was assamble and analyze through the use of the Pandas and Matplotlib Python libraries.

Finaly, four weather variables were identified: Max Temperature, Humidity, Cloudiness, and Wind Speed and plot vs tbe latitude of the cities.

This dashboard shows the analyzed data and the visualizations based on each weather variable, it implies that the best cities to travel are the one closer to the equator.
